About Us

Learn more about Eagle Concrete Coatings L.L.C.

Elevating Standards in Concrete Finishing

At Eagle Concrete Coatings, founded by Colton Kunkel, we believe your floors should be as tough as an eagle's grip and visually stunning. We specialize in transforming dull, worn out concrete into resilient, eye-catching surfaces that stand the test of time.

Whether it’s a residential garage, a commercial showroom, or outdoor staircases, our mission is to provide an elite level of workmanship. Backed by many years of experience, our team is fully licensed and insured, so you can trust we know exactly how to handle your project with precision.

  • Fully licensed and insured
  • Many years of proven experience
  • Locally owned and operated
  • Professional surface preparation
